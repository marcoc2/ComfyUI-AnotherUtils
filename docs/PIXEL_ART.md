# 🎨 Pixel Art Tools

A dedicated suite of nodes for transforming and standardizing images into pixel art formats.

#### Pixel Art Normalizer
Normalizes images into a consistent pixel art style.
- **Features:** Automatic grid detection, color quantization.
- **Outputs:** Normalized image, detected block size, true 1:1 pixel art.

#### Pixel Art Converter
Advanced single-image pixel art conversion with customizable palette and resolution settings.

#### Pixel Art Converter (Parallel)
Multi-threaded version of the pixel art converter for fast batch processing.
